Terms & Conditions - Refund Policy

ORSOLUM ORGANIC SOLUTIONS believes in helping its customers as far as possible, and has therefore a liberal cancellation policy. Under this policy:

1. Order Cancellation Policy

Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made immediately after placing the order. However, the cancellation request may not be entertained if the orders have been communicated to the vendors/merchants and they have initiated the process of shipping them.

2. Perishable Items Policy

ORSOLUM ORGANIC SOLUTIONS does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items like flowers, eatables etc. However, refund/replacement can be made if the customer establishes that the quality of product delivered is not good.

3. Damaged or Defective Products

In case of receipt of damaged or defective items please report the same to our Customer Service team. The request will, however, be entertained once the merchant has checked and determined the same at his own end.

Important: This should be reported within 15 Days of receipt of the products.

In case you feel that the product received is not as shown on the site or as per your expectations, you must bring it to the notice of our customer service within 15 Days of receiving the product. The Customer Service Team after looking into your complaint will take an appropriate decision.

4. Warranty Products

In case of complaints regarding products that come with a warranty from manufacturers, please refer the issue to them.

5. Refund Processing

In case of any Refunds approved by the ORSOLUM ORGANIC SOLUTIONS, it'll take 16-30 Days for the refund to be processed to the end customer.

Customer Service Contact

For any queries, complaints, or to report product issues, please contact our Customer Service team immediately.
